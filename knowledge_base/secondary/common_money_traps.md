# Common Money Traps for Gen Z in Germany

## Subscription Creep

- Spotify (€10.99)
- Netflix (€13–17)
- Gym (€25–50)
- iCloud/Google storage (€1–10)
- Streaming stacking effect
- “It’s only €9.99” logic adds up to €60–€100/month

## Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)

- Klarna widely used for fashion and tech
- Reduces immediate pain of spending
- Often leads to stacking small debts
- Psychological effect: separates purchase from payment

## Lifestyle Inflation After Payday

- First 5–7 days: spending spike
- More eating out, delivery, online shopping
- Emotional reward behavior

## Social Comparison

- Travel posts
- Festival culture
- “Everyone is investing except me” feeling

## Micro-Leaks

- Coffee €3–4 daily
- Food delivery convenience fees
- Impulse Amazon purchases
- Forgotten subscriptions

## Emotional Drivers

- FOMO
- Reward after stressful week
- Peer influence
- Desire for independence
