## Key Spending Insights Across Profiles

- **Subscriptions** range from €11–€32/month — often forgotten or underused
- **Eating out + coffee** is the #1 discretionary spend (€95–€120/month)
- **Savings rate** is 0–12% — most have no system, just "whatever's left"
- **Investing:** 0/3 actively invest, 1/3 curious but stuck
- **BNPL (Klarna/PayPal Later):** used by youngest profile, creates hidden debt
- **"Remaining" money** vanishes into untracked micro-purchases
